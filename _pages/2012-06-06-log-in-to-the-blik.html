Before you can login, you must have already registered for an account on the Bliki. Once you have an account, you can login from any page on the Bliki.

  1. Scroll down to the right side of any Bliki page where you see the Members menu and then enter your username and password.  Alternatively, you can select Log In along the upper administrative bar.

     

  2. Use the top administration bar, accessible from any Bliki page, to add new posts or get to the Dashboard.
