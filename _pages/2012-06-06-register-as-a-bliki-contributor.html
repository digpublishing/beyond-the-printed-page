You must first register to contribute to the Museum Digital Publishing Bliki.  Don't worry, registering couldn't be easier!

1. Scroll down to the right side of any Bliki page where you see the Members menu and the "create an account" option or along the top of any Bliki page, select Sign Up.

2. On the Create an Account page, enter your desired Bliki username, a valid email address as well as a password.  You will not be able to log into the bliki until you have activated your account from your email.

3. You will receive an email from the Bliki with the subject line of: {Beyond the Printed Page: Museum Digital Publishing Bliki}Activate Your Account. Click on the link in the email and your account will be activated.

4.When you login for the first time, go to the topic menu to select My Account and then Profile.

 

 

5. Upload an avatar and edit your profile if you so desire.

6. Use the top administration bar, accessible from any Bliki page, to add new posts or to get to the Dashboard.
